Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Law of Attraction Toolkit ("the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

The app collects the following types of information:

  • Anonymous usage data (e.g. which features and processes you use, session duration) collected via PostHog, a third-party analytics platform. This data is not linked to your identity.
  • Device and app diagnostics such as app version and device type, collected anonymously to help us improve the app.
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting support (e.g. your email address).

We do not collect your name, location, financial information, or any sensitive personal data.

How we use your information

  • To understand how users interact with the app and improve features over time
  • To test and refine the app experience using feature flags and A/B testing
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

The app uses PostHog for anonymous analytics, feature flags, and A/B testing. PostHog collects anonymized usage events and does not receive any personally identifiable information from this app. You can learn more about PostHog's privacy practices at posthog.com/privacy.

Data retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained per PostHog's standard data retention policy. Support emails are retained only as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
