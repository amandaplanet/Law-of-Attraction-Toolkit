Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Law of Attraction Toolkit ("the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

The app may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data (e.g. which features you use, session duration) collected anonymously through Apple's standard analytics, if you have opted in on your device.
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting support (e.g. your email address).

We do not collect your name, location, financial information, or any sensitive personal data through the app itself.

How we use your information

  • To improve app performance and features
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

The app does not currently integrate third-party advertising or analytics SDKs beyond Apple's platform tools.

Data retention

Support emails are retained only as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry. Anonymous usage data is retained per Apple's standard policies.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
