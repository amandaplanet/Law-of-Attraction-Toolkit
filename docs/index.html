Features

Everything you need in one place

Each tool in the app is grounded in Abraham-Hicks methodology, designed to help you shift your vibration and align with what you want.

📖

Book of Positive Aspects

Write and revisit what you appreciate — entries save automatically as you type.

🎯

Focus Wheel

Build momentum with 12 stepping-stone thoughts toward any centre statement.

🧘

Meditation Timer

Timed sessions with ambient sound options and a Lock Screen Now Playing widget.

🌈

Emotional Guidance Scale

Find where you are on the scale and get personalised process recommendations.

⏱️

68-Second Focus

Hold a pure thought for 68 seconds and send it into motion.

🍽️

Placemat Process

Hand off what isn't yours to carry and free up your energy for what matters.
