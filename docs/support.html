Frequently asked questions

How do I access the meditation and audio content?

Open the app and tap Meditation from the home screen. Select a duration and ambient sound, then tap Begin. Make sure your device volume is turned up and that silent mode is off.

The audio stops when my screen locks. How do I fix this?

Go to iPhone Settings → Display & Brightness → Auto-Lock and set it to a longer interval while listening, or keep the app open in the foreground. The app also supports background audio — make sure you haven't swiped it away from the app switcher.

I found a bug or something isn't working correctly.

Please email us at white.m.amanda@gmail.com with a description of what happened, along with your iPhone model and iOS version. Screenshots are always helpful.
